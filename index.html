The Standard
You Deserve.

We founded Knightsbury on one principle: the standard of work should never be compromised for the sake of speed or margin. In an industry where corners are routinely cut, we built something different.

Every project — from a single feature wall to a complete period property restoration — receives the same meticulous preparation and precision finish. We use only premium materials. We leave every site immaculate. We finish when we say we will finish.

What We Do

A Complete Decorative
Finishing Service

From a single room refresh to a full property transformation — we bring the same care, preparation, and precision to every project we undertake.

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, woodwork and radiators — finished to a standard that makes every room feel genuinely new.

Exterior Painting

House walls, window frames, doors and fascias — with preparation that ensures the finish lasts, not just looks good initially.
