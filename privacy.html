Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including your name, phone number, email address, postcode, and project details submitted via our contact form. This information is used solely to respond to your enquiry and provide a quote for our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to respond to enquiries, provide quotes, schedule site visits, and communicate about ongoing projects. We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is used only by Knightsbury for the purposes of responding to your enquiry and delivering our services.

Contact Form Submissions

When you submit our contact form, your information is sent via Netlify Forms and stored securely. We receive a notification and respond to your enquiry within 24 hours.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To make such a request, please contact us at hello@knightsbury.co.uk.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies. We use only essential, functional cookies necessary for the site to operate correctly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@knightsbury.co.uk.
