What We Do

Complete painting and decorating — every surface, every finish, done properly.

01 — Interior

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, skirting boards, door frames and radiators — finished to a standard that makes every room feel genuinely new. We use quality paints from trusted trade manufacturers and apply them with the preparation and care that ensures a finish which lasts.

  • All rooms: walls, ceilings, skirtings, door frames, window cills
  • Radiator painting with heat-resistant eggshell systems
  • Full preparation: filling, sanding, priming — no step skipped
  • Eggshell, satin, matte — expert advice on the right finish for every surface
  • Site left clean and tidy every single day

02 — Exterior

Exterior Painting

Your property's exterior is the first impression it makes. We prepare surfaces properly — scraping, sanding, knotting, priming — so the paint adheres correctly and withstands the British weather rather than peeling within a year.

  • House walls: render, brick, timber cladding
  • Window frames, doors and door frames
  • Fascias, soffits, bargeboards and guttering
  • Full preparation: power washing, scraping, knotting, priming
  • Weather-resistant systems from quality trade manufacturers
